# basishacks-2026-project
My project connects to “Beneath the Surface” by showing the hidden emotions behind social media posts. It helps users look past happy or normal online appearances and build empathy for feelings like stress, sadness, insecurity, or loneliness.
features: 
-users can view social media-style posts.
-the app shows what each post looks like on the surface.
-the app reveals possible hidden emotions beneath the post.
-the app gives advice to encourage empathy and support.
technologies used:
-code.org app lab
-javascript
-github
Public demo URL

How to use:
1.Open the Code.org app link.
2.Click through the app.
3.Read or select a social media-style post.
4.See the surface meaning and hidden emotion.
5.Read the advice and reflect on the deeper meaning behind online posts.
